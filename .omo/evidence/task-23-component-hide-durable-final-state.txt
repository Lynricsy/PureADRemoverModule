## Final behavior
- Android `apply_component` keeps ledger-before-mutation by appending provisional component record before mutation.
- Android `apply_component` appends confirmed component record with `hide_status=applied` after successful `pm hide` and before `pm disable-user`.
- If confirmed append fails after successful `pm hide`, the operation returns an error and does not report/continue as safely applied.
- Failure/unavailable TryHide path keeps durable `hide_status=skipped_unavailable` and restore does not call `pm unhide`, while still enabling the component.
- CLI `profile-restore` folds component provisional+confirmed entries by logical key (`kind`, `rule_id`, `package`, `component`) and restores the final state once per logical component.
- `rewrite_restored` marks only entries covered by successful restore actions and preserves other pending/future records.
